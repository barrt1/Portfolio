Marzano's Restaurant

"Since opening in 1988, Marzano has been treating South Sound diners to our interpretation of Italian cuisine based
solidly on traditions of the Veneto region in Northern Italy." -owners Elisa and Brian Marzano With an overall Trip Advisor rating
of 4.5 stars, Marzanos has got to be on your must-do list.


Duke's Chowder House

“If you're looking for a welcoming atmosphere, great service, and incredible food, you've come to the right place.” - Duke
Also known as the “Tastiest seafood in town”, Duke's food also comes from 100% sustainable sources.
Enjoy your meal while sitting on Lake Union, and enjoy half off bottles of wine every Monday. Rating 4.5 stars.


The Pacific Grill

Prided on being Tacomas most award-winning resatraunt, the Pacific Grill deserves your attention. "A stunning urban space, at home in any major city that is perfect for romance,
great conversation and world-class food." - unknown Also earing 4.5 stars, make sure you check out their wide variety of eats.


The Swiss Restaurant & Pub

“You're a stranger here but once” -The Swiss This is more than just a restaraunt and pub, this is a place to gather.
With minors welcome until 9pm, The Swiss holds events regularly and is loved by the locals.
Their extensive food and drink menu will bring you in, and you'll want to stay for another round of pool. Rating 4 stars on Trip Advisor.

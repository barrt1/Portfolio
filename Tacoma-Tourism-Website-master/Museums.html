Museum of Glass

1801 Dock Street

Tacoma, WA 98402

Phone: (253) 284-4750

Hours:

Wednesday-Saturday

10am-5pm

Sunday

12-5pm

The Museum of Glass features glass artworks from various artists.
Most prominently featured, are the works of Dale Chihuly!

They have an indoor exhibit of Chihuly's work as well as an out outdoor exhibit on the bridge of glass
where you can see them prominently displayed in glass cases as well as above you

The museum also features a glass workshop called "The Hot Shop" where you can watch the artists in action.

After watching some live glass-blowing, you can grab some refreshments and a bite to eat at the cafe.

For More Information Visit: Musuem of Glass
